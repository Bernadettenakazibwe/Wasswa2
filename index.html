Who are We!

We are fighting child hunger head-on, ensuring evry child receives a nutrious school meal. everyday.
At One Young Generation, no child in Mpigu goes hungry at school. We provide life changing school meals, empowering young minds and brighter future.
We oofer lunch meals to school going childen in rural areas. In order to make the service affordable to theparents, we offer 50% contribution of the school meals fee, thereby increasing the chances of children accessing lunch at school.
By reducing meal costs, for parents , their children are able to concentrate in classes and reduce the rateof dropouts by 20% hence reducing hunger by 30%.

By nurtuting Young bodies , we empower children in Mpigi to reach their full potential.
